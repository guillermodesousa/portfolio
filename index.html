An engineered print design for a motocross practice shirt.
Ornamentals is a seasonal catalog of prints and patterns, aimed at the fashion, textile and home design industries. It's a collection of trend-councious, ready-to-use motifs.

The designs are organized in four themes, each responding to a different inspiration source. The book can be used as a trend guide for the design process, but its main purpose is to offer versatile and high-quality prints for production.
